Interactive Projects

3D Scan Stations

A pair of touch-screen kiosk applications for exploring 3D scans of a brain and a fossilized skeleton of two dinosaurs. Features include rotatable 3D models with labelled features, multi-touch and single-touch zooming, and multiple language options.

Part of the Antarctic Dinosaurs exhibit developed by the Field Museum of Natural History. (2018)
