Interactive Projects

Permian Food Web

Field Museum of Natural History, 2019
Role: UI Designer and Software Developer

A touch-screen interactive about food webs and how the loss of ecosystem components can cause cascading extinctions. This Electron application uses D3.js to generate an animated, touch-responsive graph of animal groups and their food sources.

Antarctic Dinosaurs 3D Scan Stations

Field Museum of Natural History, 2018
Role: UI Designer and Software Developer

A pair of touch-screen kiosk applications that allow visitors to rotate and explore the 3D scans of a dinosaur brain and a dinosaur skeleton.

SUE Fast Facts

Field Museum of Natural History, 2018
Role: UI Designer and Software Developer

A railing-style touch screen featuring facts, photos, and 3D media about SUE the T. rex. Visitors can browse photo slideshows and rotate 3D models of SUE's skeleton.
